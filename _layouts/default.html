Kilian Haefeli

I will be at University of Toronto starting January 2024, reach out if you want to talk Diffusion Models, NLP, Alignment or any of my other previous endeavours.

Currently I am interning at Aleph Alpha building an application using a variety of methods for Retrieval Augmented Generation, Fine-Tuning and Guided Generation.

Also, I am studying towards a Masters degree at ETH Zurich in majoring in Machine Intelligence and Signal Processing. Previously I have finished my bachelors in 2021 at ETH Zurich in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology.

From 2019 to 2021 I co-founded and worked at a Startup called airica before selling it to Logitech in 2021, where I continued to work as a Junior Data Scientist for 4 months.
